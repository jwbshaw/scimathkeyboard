What Is SciMath?

SciMath is a keyboard for iOS and iPadOS that gives you the symbols science, mathematics, and technical writing actually need — without copying from character maps, switching apps, or approximating.

Every symbol is a proper Unicode character. It copies, searches, and renders correctly in every app that supports Unicode text.

SciMath is a one-time purchase. There is no subscription, no in-app purchase, and no advertising of any kind.

Privacy

SciMath does not require Full Access and will never ask for it. It never logs keystrokes, never makes network requests, and contains no analytics or telemetry of any kind. Nothing you type ever leaves your device. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
