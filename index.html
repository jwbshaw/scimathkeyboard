What is SciMath?

SciMath is a custom iOS and iPadOS keyboard extension providing a full QWERTY layout plus three additional symbol layers: numbers and punctuation, extended symbols, and scientific/mathematical characters.

Designed for scientists, mathematicians, engineers, students, and anyone who regularly types symbols that standard keyboards don't provide.

Privacy

SciMath does not require Full Access. It never logs keystrokes, never makes network requests, and contains no analytics or telemetry of any kind. Nothing you type ever leaves your device. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
